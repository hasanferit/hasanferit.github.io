Hasan Ferit Eniser

PhD Student @ MPI-SWS
hfeniser@mpi-sws.org

Research Interests:
Software Testing/Verification & Artificial Intelligence

Find me on Google Scholar and Twitter.
 
Tweets by @hfeniser

I am a third year Ph.D. student at MPI-SWS in Germany. Broadly, I work in the intersection of software testing/verification and artificial intelligence. I develop new testing and verification techniques or adapt existing ones to ensure the dependability of AI-enabled systems. I am advised by Dr. Maria Christakis and work in the "Practical Formal Methods" group at MPI-SWS. I graduated from Bogazici University in 2017 with a M.Sc. in computer science. Before that, I did my B.Sc. studies in computer science at the same university.

I am looking for internship opportunities for the next summer.
